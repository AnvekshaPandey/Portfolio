Urbana Road Safety Intelligence App

Python · Altair · Streamlit · Pandas · Hugging Face
“Urbana is a small city, and as someone who moved here for graduate school, I wanted to understand it better. When I found 10 years of traffic stop data sitting on a public government portal, I saw a chance to turn raw records into something genuinely useful for the community.”
10 yrsCity Data Analyzed
4Linked Visualizations
LiveDeployed on HF Spaces

An interactive analytics dashboard built on a decade of Urbana traffic-stop records, surfacing patterns across age, race, violation type, and crime category over time.

I cleaned and modeled ten years of messy public records into a tidy analytical dataset, then designed four linked Altair visualizations that update together as users explore filters.

The app makes civic data approachable — letting residents and researchers spot trends in stops by demographic, time of day, and crime category without writing a single line of code.

F1 Podium Predictor

Python · XGBoost · Scikit-learn · Feature Engineering · Hugging Face
“Every F1 fan has an opinion on who will podium. I wanted to see if seven seasons of race data — grid position, recovery ability, circuit overtaking rates — could actually do better than a guess.”
125+Races in Training Set
0.663Final F1 Score
LiveHF Prediction UI

A machine-learning app that predicts the probability of a Formula 1 driver finishing on the podium based on race-weekend and historical features.

I engineered features from seven seasons of F1 data — grid position, constructor points, circuit overtaking rate, driver recovery rate, and team trajectory — then trained an XGBoost classifier reaching a 0.663 F1 score.

The deployed Hugging Face app lets fans tweak race-setup inputs and instantly see the model’s podium-probability prediction.
